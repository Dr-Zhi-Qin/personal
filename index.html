Zhi Qin (秦智)

Postdoctoral Research Associate

Shandong University

Zhi Qin received his Bachelor’s degree in Thermal Energy and Power Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology (Weihai) in 2010. Afterward, he joined Professor Linhua Liu’s group in Power Engineering and Engineering Thermophysics at Harbin Institute of Technology and obtained his master degree in Jul. 2016 and obtained his PhD degree in Oct. 2019. He is now working with Professor Linhua Liu at Shandong University as a postdoctoral research associate.

Zhi Qin's research is related to molecular spectroscopy, electronic structures, gas radiation.
