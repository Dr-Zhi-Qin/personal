Zhi Qin (秦智)

Postdoctoral Research Associate

Shandong University

Welcome to visit my website.

Zhi Qin received his Bachelor’s degree in Thermal Energy and Power Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology (Weihai) in 2010. Afterward, he joined Professor Linhua Liu’s group in Power Engineering and Engineering Thermophysics at Harbin Institute of Technology and obtained his master degree in Jul. 2016 and subsequently got his PhD degree in Oct. 2019. He is now working with Professor Linhua Liu at Shandong University as a postdoctoral research associate.

Zhi Qin's research is related to quantumn chemistry calculations, molecular spectroscopy, molecular dynamics, gas radiation. He works on theoretical spectroscopy and then applications in astrophysics. He is now working on the calculations of radiative association and photodissociation of diatomic molecules for applications to astrochemistry.
